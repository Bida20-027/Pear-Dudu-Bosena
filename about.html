Flipside

Embrace eccentricity and get wacky at Esplanade's ten-day playful festival, where a host of whimsical programmes awaits. However, due to the current restrictions, some programmes have been cancelled. Nonetheless, there's still an exciting line-up of free programmes online.

Concerts for Children

It's never too early to introduce the kids to the magic of music. And what better way to kickstart the mid-year school holidays than treating them to a colourful combination of classical music, engaging animation, and captivating storytelling?

Summer Art Jam

Save the pool for another day, and make a dive for a summer celebration of creativity at The Substation. The Summer Art Jam pop-up is presented by Miaja Art Collections and comes as part of the La Piscine solo exhibition by Singapore-based artist Aude Gooly.

Gallery Children’s Biennale

Got a mini Picasso at home? Unlock your child's creativity with a multi-sensorial art experience in the first-ever "phygital" edition of the Gallery Children's Biennale. It's set to launch an online experience via a microsite on May 22.
